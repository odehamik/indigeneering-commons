## **MIKINAAK *OMITIGWAPISHIMONIKE* GAYE**

## **SNAPPING-TURTLE AND CADDICE-FLY

1)  > **Ningoding sa giiwenh odetoowag gakina endaswewaanagizid - mikinaak, detabikinaak, boozikado, miskwaadesi; mii iw gakina ezhi-maamawi-oodetoowaad. Wiin idash mikinaak ogimaawi.**


<div class="four-layer">

> <span class="l-eng">*Now, once on a time they say there was a town of every kind (of turtle) that was, — a Snapping-Turtle, a Soft-Shelled Turtle, a Musk-Turtle, a Painted-Turtle; thus the total number of them that lived together in a town.*</span>

</div>


Ningoding sa giiwenh (now, once on a time, they say) odetoowag (they had a town) gakina endaswewaanagizid (every kind there was) — mikinaak (Snapping-Turtle), detabikinaak (Soft-Shelled Turtle), boozikado (Musk-Turtle), miskwaadesi (Painted-Turtle); mii iw gakina (so the whole number of them) ezhi-maamawi-oodetoowaad (that dwelt together in one town). Wiin idash mikinaak (and he, Snapping-Turtle) ogimaawi (was the chief).

2)  > **Ningoding idash mikinaak inaakonige wii-andobanid; *omitigwapishimoniken* owii-awi-miigaanaan.**


<div class="four-layer">

> <span class="l-eng">*So once on a time Snapping-Turtle announced that he planned to go to war; against Caddice-Fly was he going to fight.*</span>

</div>


Ningoding idash mikinaak (so once on a time Snapping-Turtle) inaakonige (decided, gave the word) wii-andobanid (that he would go to war); omitigwapishimoniken (Caddice-Fly) owii-awi-miigaanaan (he was going to go and fight him).

3)  > **Mii zhigwa ozhiitaawaad wii-andobaniwaad; gii-gichi-manidookaazo mikinaak.**


<div class="four-layer">

> <span class="l-eng">*Thereupon they then made ready to go to war; greatly did Snapping-Turtle conjure for magic power.*</span>

</div>


Mii zhigwa (thereupon now) ozhiitaawaad (they made ready) wii-andobaniwaad (to go to war); gii-gichi-manidookaazo mikinaak (greatly did Snapping-Turtle conjure for spirit-power).

4)  > **Apii waa-andobanid gii-gichi-inendam gaye.**


<div class="four-layer">

> <span class="l-eng">*At the time when setting out for war, very proud was he too.*</span>

</div>


Apii waa-andobanid (at the time he was setting out for war) gii-gichi-inendam gaye (very proud was he too).

11) > **Mii dash gii-maajaawaad aapiji niibiwa owiijiiwaan odooshkiniigima'.**


<div class="four-layer">

Mii dash gii-maajaawaad (and so when they started off) aapiji niibiwa (very many) owiijiiwaan (he had going with him) odooshkiniigima’ (his young men).

12) > **Apii dash wedisaad *omitigwapishimoniken* oodetoonid, bizhishig bagamaaganan odakonaanaawaan; gaawiin bakaan gegoo ogii-ayaasiinaawaa, mii eta go bagamaaganan.**


<div class="four-layer">

</div>


Apii dash wedisaad (and when he reached) omitigwapishimoniken oodetoonid (where Caddice-Fly had his town), bizhishig bagamaaganan (nothing but war-clubs) odakonaanaawaan (did they hold in their hands); gaawiin bakaan gegoo (nothing else of any kind) ogii-ayaasiinaawaa (did they have), mii eta go bagamaaganan (only war-clubs).

13) > **Mii idash zhigwa mawinadamowaad i'iw oodena, *mitigwapishimonish* odoodena, mii iwe miigaadiwaad.**


<div class="four-layer">

> <span class="l-eng">*Accordingly, when they rushed to attack the town, the town of Caddice-Fly, then did they fight with (the Caddice-Flies).*</span>

</div>


Mii idash zhigwa (and accordingly now) mawinadamowaad (when they rushed upon) i’iw oodena (that town), mitigwapishimonish odoodena (the town of the Caddice-Flies), mii iwe miigaadiwaad (then it was that they fought one another).

14) > **Giishpin awiya nisind boodaanidiwag mii i'w miinawaa bimaadiziwaad; gaye dash baapaasaganaandiwag mii go gaye iw endoodaadiwaad, boodaanidiwag.**


<div class="four-layer">

> <span class="l-eng">*When any one was slain, they breathed upon him, whereupon back to life he came; and if they had their shells cracked, then the same thing they did to one another, they breathed upon one another.*</span>

</div>


Giishpin awiya nisind (if anyone was killed) boodaanidiwag (they breathed upon one another) mii i’w miinawaa bimaadiziwaad (and so back to life they came); gaye dash baapaasaganaandiwag (and if their shells were cracked) mii go gaye iw endoodaadiwaad (the same thing too they did to one another), boodaanidiwag (they breathed upon one another).

15) > **Gaye iniw *mitigwapishimonish* odooshkiniigima' endoodaminid awiya biigwaganaamind, boodaanidiwag; mii dash neyaab naabisewaad ezhinaagoziwaad.**


<div class="four-layer">

> <span class="l-eng">*The same, too, did the youths of Caddice-Fly whenever any one was torn to pieces, they breathed upon him; whereupon they would take their places, looking the same as before.*</span>

</div>


Gaye iniw mitigwapishimonish odooshkiniigima’ (the same too the young men of Caddice-Fly) endoodaminid awiya biigwaganaamind (did whenever anyone was beaten to pieces), boodaanidiwag (they breathed upon one another); mii dash neyaab naabisewaad (and so back they would take their places) ezhinaagoziwaad (looking as they had before).

16) > **Apii eni-naawakweg, mii iw zhigwa zhaagooji'ind mikinaak; gaawiin gashki'idiziiwag ji-bimaaji'idiwaad, aapiji gichi-miigaadiwag.**


<div class="four-layer">

> <span class="l-eng">*When it was getting well on towards noon, then was Snapping-Turtle being overcome; (his youths) were becoming unable to bring one another back to life again, very hard were they fighting one another.*</span>

</div>


Apii eni-naawakweg (when it was getting on toward noon), mii iw zhigwa zhaagooji’ind mikinaak (then now was Snapping-Turtle being overcome); gaawiin gashki’idiziiwag (they were no longer able) ji-bimaaji’idiwaad (to bring one another back to life), aapiji gichi-miigaadiwag (very hard were they fighting one another).

<div class="four-layer">

> <span class="l-eng">*In the end all his youths were slain; only Snapping-Turtle himself was not slain, he was taken captive.*</span>

</div>


Gegapii gakina gii-nisimaa odoshkiniigima’ (in the end all his young men were slain); wiin eta mikinaak (only Snapping-Turtle himself) gaawiin gii-nisaasii (was not killed), gii-dakonaa (he was taken captive).

Mii dash imaa babaamosewaad mitaawangaang (and then there they walked along the sandy beach).
